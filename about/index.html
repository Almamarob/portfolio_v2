Who I Am

Nice to meet you, I'm Alessandra!

I’m a bit of a hybrid: a Licensed Civil Engineer who found her true calling in Digital Product Management.

I’ve never been satisfied with just knowing how buildings stand up; I’ve always wanted to improve how people live in and manage them. This curiosity led me to trade construction sites and fire safety simulations for the fast-paced world of B2B and B2C digital platforms.

What do I bring to the table? The rigor of engineering paired with the vision of product development. I am the "Technical Bridge", the person who speaks the language of developers, stakeholders, and industry experts without needing a translator.

I’m the one who asks "why" a dozen times, not to be difficult, but to ensure we’re building the right solution for the right problem. I believe that even the most complex business logic deserves a design that feels human and effortless.

I’m a big believer in virtuous cycles, it’s not just about meeting requirements, but about designing systems that actually feed themselves, where data, people, and resources work together to cut out waste and build something that lasts.

Today, I enjoy combining these two worlds to build digital products that improve how cities operate, how assets are managed, and how people interact with the built environment.
