Who I Am

Nice to meet you, I'm Alessandra!

I'm drawn to one question, wherever I find it: does this actually hold up?

I started asking it as a Licensed Civil Engineer, and I've never stopped, testing how systems perform in the real world, not just on paper. Does it hold its weight? Does it create value?

I don't settle for the surface, learning is what drives everything I do. I look for the logic underneath, the reason something works, I untangle it, put it in order, and make it visible. I help others see it too.

What do I bring to the table? The rigor of engineering paired with the clarity of structured analysis. I am the person who speaks the language of stakeholders and industry experts without needing a translator.

I'm the one who asks "why" a dozen times, not to be difficult, but because I don't stop at the first plausible answer. I keep digging until I find the real one, and every discovery along the way gives me something back.

I believe every system, every project, deserves an answer that's clear, verifiable, and honest about its risks: built on virtuous cycles, where data, people, and resources work together, with nothing wasted.

Today, I bring these worlds together: reading systems, weighing risk, and shaping solutions that change how the built environment serves the people who live in it.
